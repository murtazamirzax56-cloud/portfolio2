03 — Process

How I design.

A structured, iterative framework that keeps the user at the center of every decision.

  1. 01

    Discover

    Understand the problem, the users, and the context through research and observation.

  2. 02

    Define

    Synthesize insights into clear goals, personas, and problem statements.

  3. 03

    Ideate

    Explore a wide range of solutions through sketches, flows, and brainstorming.

  4. 04

    Prototype

    Build interactive mockups that bring ideas to life and invite feedback.

  5. 05

    Test

    Validate with real users, measure what works, and uncover what doesn't.

  6. 06

    Improve

    Iterate relentlessly. Great design is never finished — only refined.
